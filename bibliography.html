Biography

By: Aaryan Panchal

Aaryan D Panchal was born on January 19, 2011, in Nashua. He is currently a ninth-grade student at Lexington High School in Lexington, Massachusetts. Raised by supportive and kind parents. His mother working in biotech and his father in software engineering. Aaryan has been encouraged to explore a wide range of interests and develop his own path.

From an early age, Aaryan has been curious and driven. After attending Academy of Notre Dame, he moved to Lexington Public Schools, where he learned the value of discipline and dedication, especially after eighth grade. This self-discipline has helped him pursue diverse interests, such as playing the guitar, where he has learned several songs including "Merry Go Round of Life" from the film Howl's Moving Castle.
